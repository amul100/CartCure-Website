How to Create a Backup of Your Theme

Creating a backup of your Shopify theme ensures you can restore your store to its current state if needed. We recommend doing this before any changes are made to your store.

Method 1: Duplicate Your Theme (Recommended)

This is the quickest and easiest way to create a backup:

Step 1: Go to Online Store

From your Shopify admin, click Online Store in the left sidebar, then click Themes.

Step 2: Find Your Current Theme

Your current (live) theme will be shown at the top under "Current theme".

Step 3: Duplicate the Theme

Click the ... (three dots) button next to your theme name, then select Duplicate.

Step 4: Rename the Backup

Once duplicated, click the ... button on the copy and select Rename. Give it a name like "Backup - [Today's Date]" so you can easily identify it later.

Tip: The duplicate will appear in your "Theme library" section below your current theme. Keep it there until your project is complete and you're happy with the changes.

Method 2: Download Your Theme Files

For an extra layer of protection, you can also download your theme files to your computer:

Step 1: Go to Themes

From your Shopify admin, go to Online Store → Themes.

Step 2: Download the Theme

Click the ... button next to your theme, then select Download theme file.

Step 3: Save the File

Shopify will email you a download link. Save this .zip file to your computer in a safe location.

How to Create a Staff Account for CartCure

Creating a staff account gives us the access we need to work on your store, while keeping your main login secure. You can easily remove this access once the project is complete.

Why use a staff account? Staff accounts are more secure than sharing your main login. They have limited permissions, can be monitored, and can be instantly revoked when the work is done.

Creating the Staff Account

Step 1: Go to Settings

From your Shopify admin, click Settings (gear icon) in the bottom left corner.

Step 2: Open Users and Permissions

Click Users and permissions from the settings menu.

Step 3: Add Staff

Click the Add staff button.

Step 4: Enter CartCure Details

Enter the following information:

  • First name: CartCure
  • Last name: Support
  • Email: info@cartcure.co.nz

Step 5: Set Permissions

Select the permissions we'll need. For most projects, we recommend:

  • Home: View
  • Products: View and edit (if product changes are needed)
  • Online Store: View and edit themes, pages, navigation, blog posts
  • Settings: View (if we need to check settings)

If you're unsure, you can grant full access and we'll only use what we need.

Step 6: Send Invitation

Click Send invite. We'll receive an email to accept the invitation and can then access your store.

Removing Access After the Project

Once your project is complete and you're happy with the work:

Step 1: Go to Users and Permissions

From Settings, click Users and permissions.

Step 2: Remove the Staff Account

Find "CartCure Support" in the list, click on it, then click Remove staff.

Important: We recommend removing staff access within a week of project completion. This is a good security practice for any third-party access to your store.

Need Help?

If you have trouble with any of these steps, don't worry! Just let us know when you submit your request and we can guide you through the process or suggest alternative access methods.
